About The NR8iv Africa

The NR8iv Africa is a story development agency committed to telling bold and authentic African stories for global audiences and empowering African storytellers to shine globally through its platform.

Led creatively by Head Writer Pearl Agwu, we are building a slate of stories and films that are unapologetically African, rigorously crafted, and globally resonant.
